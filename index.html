Quick Insight: Why VXLAN?

Standard VLANs are limited to 4,096 IDs. In a massive cloud environment, that's nothing. VXLAN expands this to 16 million IDs and lets you stretch Layer 2 across any Layer 3 network—making workload mobility a reality.
